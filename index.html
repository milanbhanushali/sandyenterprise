Frequently Asked Questions

Sure, here are some frequently asked questions (FAQs) about logistics delivery of parcels and their corresponding answers:

  • What happens if I'm not available to receive my parcel?

    The logistics company will attempt to deliver your parcel a few more times, usually on different days. If they are unable to deliver your parcel after multiple attempts, they may hold it at a nearby location for you to pick up or return it to the sender.

  • How can I track my parcel?

    You can track your parcel using the tracking number provided by the logistics company. This number can usually be found in your order confirmation email or on the logistics company's website.

  • How much will shipping cost?

    Shipping costs vary depending on various factors such as the weight and size of the parcel, the shipping method, and the destination. You can usually find the shipping cost on the logistics company's website or by contacting their customer service.

  • Can I request a specific delivery time?

    It depends on the logistics company's policies. Some companies offer specific delivery times for an additional fee, while others do not. You can contact the logistics company's customer service for more information.
